# Lumora Support

Need help? We'd love to hear from you.

## Contact
**Email:** support@lumora.app
**Response time:** within 48 hours

## Common questions

### How do I cancel my subscription?
iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Lumora → Cancel Subscription.

### How do I delete my account?
Profile tab → Delete Account. All your data is removed within 30 days.

### My story isn't playing
1. Check your internet connection
2. Force-quit and reopen Lumora
3. If the problem persists, email us with a screenshot

### How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Paywall → "Restore Purchases" at the bottom.
